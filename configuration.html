配置

PHPUnit

build.xml 文件里的 phpunit 任务 (see "自动化") 默认的PHPUNIT XML配置文件目标如下：

<logging>
 <log type="coverage-html" target="build/coverage"/>
 <log type="coverage-clover" target="build/logs/clover.xml"/>
 <log type="coverage-crap4j" target="build/logs/crap4j.xml"/>
 <log type="junit" target="build/logs/junit.xml" logIncompleteSkipped="false"/>
</logging>

下载 一份 phpunit.xml.dist 放到你的项目根目录，就可以开始了

阅读PHPUnit文档获取更多详情.

phpDox

build.xml文件里的 phpdox 任务 (see "自动化") 默认的XML配置文件:

<phpdox xmlns="http://phpdox.net/config">
 <project name="name-of-project" source="src" workdir="build/phpdox">
  <collector publiconly="false">
   <include mask="*.php" />
  </collector>

  <generator output="build">
   <build engine="html" enabled="true" output="api">
    <file extension="html" />
   </build>
  </generator>
 </project>
</phpdox>

阅读phpDox 文档获取更多详情.

PHP_CodeSniffer

build.xml 文件里的 phpcs 和 phpcs-ci 任务 (see "自动化") 默认的XML配置文件:

<ruleset name="name-of-your-coding-standard">
 <description>Description of your coding standard</description>

 <rule ref="Generic.PHP.DisallowShortOpenTag"/>
 <!-- ... -->
</ruleset>

默认构建规则视为 PHP_CodeSniffer 参数定义在 build/phpcs.xml里

阅读 PHP_CodeSniffer 文档 for 获取更多详情.

PHPMD

build.xml文件里的phpmd 和 phpmd-ci 任务 (see "自动化") 默认的XML配置文件:

<ruleset name="name-of-your-coding-standard"
  xmlns="http://pmd.sf.net/ruleset/1.0.0"
  xmlns:xsi="http://www.w3.org/2001/XMLSchema-instance"
  xsi:schemaLocation="http://pmd.sf.net/ruleset/1.0.0
                      http://pmd.sf.net/ruleset_xml_schema.xsd"
  xsi:noNamespaceSchemaLocation="http://pmd.sf.net/ruleset_xml_schema.xsd">
  <description>Description of your coding standard</description>

  <rule ref="rulesets/codesize.xml/CyclomaticComplexity" />
  <!-- ... -->
</ruleset>

默认构建规则视为 PHPMD 参数定义在 build/phpmd.xml里

阅读 PHPMD文档 for 获取更多详情.
